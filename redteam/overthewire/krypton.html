Krypton 5-7:

Well done. You’ve moved past an easy substitution cipher. The main weakness of a simple substitution cipher is repeated use of a simple key. In the previous exercise you were able to introduce arbitrary plaintext to expose the key. In this example, the cipher mechanism is not available to you, the attacker. However, you have been lucky.
