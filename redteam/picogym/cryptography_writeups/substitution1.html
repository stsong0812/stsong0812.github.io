substitution1

Description: A second message has come in the mail, and it seems almost identical to the first one. Maybe the same thing will work again. Download the message here.

Writeup: We are given an encrypted message, we can use common sense and letter frequency techniques to decrypt the message. To make the process easier we can convert all characters to upper case as our flag will be all caps we also won't have to translate both lowercase and uppercase letters.



First translate the letters that we know for sure in the first part of our flag, which we know correspond to "PICOCTF".



Looking at our translated text, we can accurately guess what some of the words will be. Only needing to decrypt the flag, it wasn't difficult to assume what the words in the flag would be. Then filling in what the rest of the flag would be based on words we could assume from the large paragraph.
